About Sanjivani

Sanjivani College of Engineering, established in 1983, is a premier institution dedicated to excellence in technical education and research. With a legacy of innovation and a commitment to holistic development, the college nurtures future-ready engineers who contribute to industry and society with skill, creativity, and integrity.

Sanjivani Techfest 2.0 celebrates this spirit through events that challenge, inspire, and connect students from diverse disciplines.
